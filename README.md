**Trade Buddy Software Update Instructions:**
1. Get a USB-C cable that can transfer data
2. Plug one end of the USB-C cable into the computer
3. While holding the button on the back of the Trade Buddy, plug the other end of the USB-C cable into the Trade Buddy (TB). The TB screen should stay blank.
4. You should see “RPI-RP2” as a storage or drive on your computer
5. Drag the update file into the “RPI-RP2” drive
6. After about 20 seconds, the TB will turn on, indicating the update is complete
7. Unplug the TB from the computer, and the TB is ready for use
